## Young Scientists' Conference on Astronomy and Space Physics (Ukraine)

Ukraine's premier international conference for bachelor's, master's, and PhD students and young postdocs — its Local Organizing Committee consists solely of students. I first took part in 2013 with a poster, joined the LOC the next year, became secretary in 2018, and chair of the High-Energy Astrophysics section in 2021.

- Chair, High-Energy Astrophysics section · *2021 – Present*
- Local Organizing Committee · *2014 – Present*
- Secretary, Local Organizing Committee · *2018 – 2020*

## Ukrainian International Cosmic Ray Day (2022 & 2023)

Hosted with DESY (2022, 2023) and the Pierre Auger Observatory (2023) for Ukrainian school students. 2022: 4 mentors, 16 participants. 2023: 5 mentors, 20 participants.

## Astronomy Battles Tournament — AstroSandbox

A nationwide Ukrainian astronomy competition for high-school and undergraduate students ([official website](https://www.astrosandbox.com/fightsenglis)). I liaise with German sponsors, help organize the event, and serve as a judge.

## DESY Ukraine Summer School 2024 & Winter School 2023

Organizer of both schools, which bring Ukrainian students to DESY for research projects, lectures, and mentoring.

## Workshop "Presentation of Scientific Results" (2021)

Main organizer of a lecture series helping Ukrainian astronomy students improve their presentation and writing skills; one lecture was integrated into the DESY Ukraine Winter School.
